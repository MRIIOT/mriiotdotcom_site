Three-tier architecture

Connector. Horizons. Zenith.

DIME is a complete data platform across three tiers. The Connector runs on the plant floor, speaking each asset's native protocol and translating it into a unified stream. Horizons manage the connectors at each site — reporting fleet health, picking up tasks, and executing locally. Zenith sits in the cloud, holding the state of the entire fleet, auto-detecting stale devices, and aggregating data from every site.

FAQ

DIME, answered.

What is DIME?

DIME (Data In Motion Enterprise) is a universal translator for industrial data. A high-performance edge connector speaks your equipment’s native protocols and turns PLCs, robots, CNCs, sensors, databases, and message brokers into one unified, normalized stream — with zero custom code.

Which protocols and devices does DIME support?

50+ industrial protocols out of the box, including Fanuc CNC and robots, Yaskawa, Siemens S7, Rockwell/Allen-Bradley EtherNet/IP, Beckhoff ADS, Modbus TCP, OPC-UA, MTConnect, MQTT, and SparkplugB — plus databases and brokers such as MSSQL, Postgres, Timescale, and Redis.

Where can DIME send my data?

To 16+ destinations — historians, brokers, databases, and analytics tools such as OPC-UA, SparkplugB, Splunk, and MongoDB. A low-code/no-code DataOps engine normalizes and contextualizes the stream first, so mismatched assets feed one coherent picture.

How is DIME architected?

Three independently deployable tiers: the Connector runs at the edge and translates native protocols; Horizon manages the connectors at each site and executes tasks locally; Zenith sits in the cloud, holds the state of the whole fleet, and aggregates data across sites. It’s built to run across unreliable networks, strict firewalls, and air-gapped environments.

How fast is it, and do I need to write code?

The edge connector runs at sub-millisecond latency, and you configure rather than program — no custom integration code.

Can AI configure DIME for me?

Yes. DIME’s AI can build a working configuration straight from your machine documentation in about a minute. DIMEBBS is the definitive technical resource and AI configuration method, with a YAML playground to validate configs.

How long until I see value?

Hours instead of weeks. DIME connects the machines, systems, and protocols you already own without ripping anything out.
