Case Study · Business Systems · Make-to-order manufacturer · Representative scenario

Stop re-typing the same order.

Every customer order was keyed by hand into three systems — so growth just meant more typing.
Orders in. Hands off.Powered by DIME & i3X

The situation

A make-to-order manufacturer's office staff re-entered every order across quoting, ERP, and shipping. Each new account multiplied the manual work — and the chances of a costly typo.

The challenge

Scaling sales meant scaling clerical headcount. The team was busy, but a lot of that effort was moving the same data from one screen to another — time that didn't add value, and mistakes that did add cost and erode customer trust.

The solution

Mr. IIoT connected the systems with DIME and i3X and automated the hand-offs — so orders flow in, route between systems, and generate the downstream paperwork without anyone re-keying. The data has one source of truth instead of three.

When the same number lives in three systems, your people become the integration. Software should do that job.

The result

  • Office staff are freed from repetitive data entry for higher-value work.
  • Orders move faster and cleaner, with fewer manual errors.
  • New customers no longer mean proportionally more clerical work.
  • The business scales sales without scaling the back office.
