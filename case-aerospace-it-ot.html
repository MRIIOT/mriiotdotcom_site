Revolutionizing IT/OT connectivity in aerospace operations

“I had a very positive experience working with Chris… He's got a real passion for his work.”— IIoT Manager

The situation

A worldwide aerospace firm needed to connect hundreds of pieces of industrial equipment to its Operational Technology (OT) network. They wanted an outside specialist who could integrate IT and OT systems for reliable data collection and analytics.

The challenge

Their previous IT vendor had fallen short on implementation and support. The firm needed someone who could not only connect disparate systems, but validate the data and improve day-to-day efficiency.

The solution

Chris Misztur, an independent IT/OT specialist, came on for a six-month contract, recommended by one of the firm's vendors. His brief:

  • Connecting hundreds of pieces of industrial equipment to the OT network.
  • Determining the necessary software and hardware requirements for effective data collection.
  • Implementing third-party solutions and validating the accuracy of the collected data.

From day one, his approach was hands-on:

  • Conducting a thorough assessment of the existing IT and OT infrastructure.
  • Engaging with team members to facilitate knowledge transfer and gather insights about specific equipment and their data requirements.
  • Developing customized software components that enabled seamless integration and data analytics across the diverse range of machinery.

The result

The work delivered clear results:

  • Successful integration of multiple equipment systems, resulting in enhanced data flow to the OT network.
  • Increased operational efficiency through real-time data collection and analytics, reducing downtime and enabling better decision-making.
  • Positive feedback from the Software Engineer Manager on his collaboration within the existing team.
“Bringing Chris on board was an excellent way to supplement the company's IT team.”— Software Engineer Manager

Conclusion

Thanks to Chris's involvement, the aerospace firm overcame its prior IT challenges without a prolonged investment in training or developing internal technical competencies — positioning the firm for future technological advancements. The firm would absolutely recommend Chris for his work ethic, knowledge, and deep expertise in IT/OT integration.
