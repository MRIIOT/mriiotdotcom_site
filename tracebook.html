Why Tracebook

Answers in seconds, not callbacks in days.

Cited answers

Every response links to the exact source document and page — no hallucinated guidance.

Multi-modal input

Customers ask with text, a photo of a fault screen, or a video clip.

Per-machine knowledge

White-label

Closed loop

How it works

From manual chaos to instant answers.

Support knowledge is scattered across PDFs, emails, and video archives, and the call always comes at the worst time. Tracebook consolidates it all into one tool, then uses AI to answer recurring diagnostic questions in under 45 seconds — cutting around-the-clock support calls and response delays while keeping a human in the loop for the hard ones.

  • Ingest manuals, schematics, videos, and resolved tickets
  • Answer diagnostic questions with cited sources in seconds
  • Escalate the unknowns — and learn from every resolution
